The Best Food Pantry at University of Michigan

What is our Mission?

Our Mission Here at the Maize and Blue Cupboard is to ensure members of the University of Michigan community—whether on a tight budget or physically restrained from getting to a grocery store—receive equitable access to healthy, nutritious, and nourishing food and the ability to prepare it for themselves or others.

Expanding on the mission of the Maize and Blue Cupboard, the initiative goes beyond simply providing food—it fosters a supportive environment where students and community members can access essential resources without stigma or barriers.

More Information!

Here at the Maize and Blue Cupboard we have a lot of resources that anyone of all ages can use whether it be utensils or groceries! We restock our supplies every Monday, Wednesday, and Thursday!

Come make an appointment now, take as much groceries as you need but make sure to leave some for others too! Make sure to arrive on time for your appointment because spots are limited!!

Donate Today!

Whether students, faculty, or staff are facing financial constraints or physical barriers to grocery shopping, your contributions help provide essential resources like fresh produce, pantry staples, and kitchen utensils. Your generosity makes a direct impact, consider making a donation today!

Donating to the Maize and Blue Cupboard is a meaningful way to support the University of Michigan community by ensuring everyone has access to healthy, nutritious food.
